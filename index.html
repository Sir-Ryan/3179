Registrations by Postcode: This visualisation displays the distribution of car registrations across different postcodes of Australia.

Fuel Consumption vs. Price: Hatchbacks consistently have the best fuel economy due to their smaller size and lighter weight. Sedans tend to have lower fuel efficiency because many are built for higher performance or sportier driving experiences, prioritising power over fuel savings. A similar observation can be made for higher priced vehicles.

Odometer vs Year of Manufacture: There is a clear trend of higher kilometre readings for cars manufactured between 2000 and 2013.
