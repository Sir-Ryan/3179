Car Price Analysis

Ryan Howarth

This page provides an analysis of car prices in Australia, showcasing various trends related to different car models, regions, and vehicle attributes. The visualisations below explore key insights, including the distribution of car types by location, car registration trends, and pricing patterns by year.

Visualisation 1: This visualisation displays the distribution of car prices across different regions of Australia. It allows for a quick comparison of how prices vary by postcode, giving insight into regional pricing differences. These trends can help buyers or sellers understand the impact of location on car prices.

Fuel Consumption vs. Price: Hatchbacks consistently have the best fuel economy due to their smaller size and lighter weight. SUVs offer decent fuel economy despite their larger size, thanks to modern engineering and fuel-saving technologies. Sedans tend to have lower fuel efficiency because many are built for higher performance or sportier driving experiences, prioritising power over fuel savings. A similar observation can be made for higher priced vehicles. This pattern suggests that vehicle design and intended use play a significant role in fuel economy outcomes.

Visualisation 3: This graph shows the distribution of car types (e.g., sedan, SUV, hatchback) and their prices across various years. It highlights pricing trends over time and the popularity of different car types, which can assist in predicting future market shifts.
